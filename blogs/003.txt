Title: Nobody knows me
Date: 2026-04-09
Type: poetry

---

Nobody knows me
What I have been going through,
Nobody knows
Nobody knew and will ever know.
Probably nobody had ever tried to...
What's it like when you've no one to talk to,
Nobody to think about, to walk with
Nobody knows.

I'm like an isolated droplet when it drizzles,
Gone wild and then lost forever.
Nobody knows where to find
Nobody cares who to mind
Because nobody knows me.
